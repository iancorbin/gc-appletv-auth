GC for Apple TV

Connect your GameChanger account

1
Open web.gc.com in Chrome on this device
2
Sign in, then open DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Opt+I)
3
Go to Network tab, click any request to api.team-manager.gc.com
4
Copy the gc-token header value
5
Paste it below and tap Connect
✓

Connected!

Return to your Apple TV. You should be signed in now.
